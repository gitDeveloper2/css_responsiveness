The Council of Law Society of Kenya, led by the president Mr. Eric Theuri, issued a press statement addressing the Memorandum issued by His Excellency the President on the proposed Constitutional Amendments. The Society stated that the proposed amendments are unconstitutional and called upon Kenyans to join the movement as it is our collective duty to uphold the Constitution and rule of Law.

LSK notes, with a lot of concern, that leaders in office are proposing constitutional amendments that touch on the root of our democracy and redirecting attention from other pressing national issues. Full statement: https://bit.ly/LSKStatement

AS THE LAW SOCIETY OF KENYA, WE ARE ASTOUNDED BY THIS MOVE BY THE PRESIDENT GIVEN THAT THE AMENDMENTS WILL AFFECT THE OVERALL STRUCTURE OF PARLIAMENT AND THE EXECUTIVE.

-ERIC THEURI, PRESIDENT OF THE LAW SOCIETY OF KENYA
